THE GOOD, THE BAD, THE UGLY (Actually, the Pretty, the Ugly, the Good)

In the problems below, you are given three data sets from which you will be creating graphs. You will be creating one of:

  • Pretty - the prettiest graph you can make

  • Ugly - the ugliest graph you can make (think beyond color, though colors will likely be choices you make)

  • Best - the best graph you can make. What makes a good graph? Clear, understandable, labeled, uncluttered. Ask for opinions from classmates and Prof Roth.

You will be making one each of Pretty and Ugly and two of Best, but you may choose which dataset gets what.
